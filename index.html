MIDI Talk in a Box

Play with your music


MIDI Talk in a Box lets you interact with your music with your MIDI launchpad. While a song is playing, you can take control, speeding it up, slowing it down, skipping beats and so on. To get started pick a song from the Gallery of songs or Upload your favorite song.


MIDI Talk in a Box relies on expermental browser features. To use this app you will need to the latest version of Google Chrome Canary. To use a MIDI device such as the Launchpad you will need to enable Web MIDI API support via chrome://flags/.

Created at Music Hack Day Boston 2014 by Paul Lamere.
